The Challenge

The team's onboarding process relied heavily on informal knowledge transfer — senior engineers walking newcomers through undocumented processes. This created inconsistency, slowed time-to-productivity, and pulled senior engineers away from delivery work every time someone new joined.

The Solution

Designed and delivered a complete onboarding infrastructure: training guides, video walkthroughs, Confluence knowledge repositories built from scratch, and presentation decks for structured induction sessions — all built for reusability and long-term maintainability.

What I Demonstrated

End-to-end project coordination from problem identification to full team adoption. Stakeholder communication across engineers, team leads, and product owners. Built maintainable knowledge systems in Confluence that the team continued using for 6+ years.

The system reduced new-hire ramp-up time by approximately 25%, measured through team lead feedback and reduction in time-to-first-contribution across 10+ engineers. The Confluence knowledge base remained the team's primary reference point across 150+ Agile sprint cycles over 6 years.
